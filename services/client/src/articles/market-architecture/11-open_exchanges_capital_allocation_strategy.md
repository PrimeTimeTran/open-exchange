### Strategic Framing: Show Me the Budget

A roadmap without a budget is a hallucination. Transparency means showing exactly how resources are deployed. We are building this exchange with an initial capital injection of $50,000. This post breaks down exactly where every dollar goes.

We are optimizing for **Survival** and **Verification**, not comfort.

### Technical & Numerical Reality

**The $50,000 Allocation:**

1.  **Liquidity Seeding ($30,000 - 60%):**

    - Direct capital deployed into the AMM pools to ensure day-one tradability. This is principal protected (mostly) but at risk of IL. It is "working assets."

2.  **Security & Audits ($10,000 - 20%):**

    - Retainer for security review and automated monitoring tools. We prioritize code safety over everything else.

3.  **Infrastructure & Operations ($5,000 - 10%):**

    - Server costs, RPC nodes, and data indexing for the public dashboards.

4.  **Legal & Compliance ($3,000 - 6%):**

    - Entity formation and basic terms of service structuring.

5.  **Contingency Reserve ($2,000 - 4%):**
    - Emergency buffer.

### Capital Implications

This budget reveals our priorities.

**Decision:**
**$0 for Marketing.**

- **Why:** We believe liquidity _is_ marketing. A working, liquid exchange attracts volume. A marketed, illiquid exchange attracts complaints.
- **Leverage:** We leverage "Proof of Work" (this blog series, our open code, our live data) as our primary marketing vehicle. We invest in the _product_, not the _promise_.
